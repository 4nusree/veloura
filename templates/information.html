Our Story

About Us

Veloura is a vision brought to life by Ameena, built on elegance, confidence, and timeless femininity. Founded in Kochi, Kerala with a passion for style and self-expression, we set out to create a destination where modern women could find ethnic wear that truly speaks to them — pieces that are as comfortable as they are breathtaking.

Every kurta, anarkali, and churidhar in our collection is sourced directly from skilled artisans across India. We work with weavers in Jaipur, embroiderers in Lucknow, and fabric specialists in Surat to bring you garments of exceptional quality and authenticity.

At Veloura, we believe fashion is a celebration — of culture, confidence, and individuality. Whether you're dressing for a festive occasion, a casual outing, or the everyday, we have the perfect piece to make you feel uniquely you.

Delivery

Shipping Policy

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days of placement. Orders placed on Sundays or public holidays are processed the next working day.

Delivery Timelines

  • Standard Delivery: 4–7 business days (pan-India)
  • Express Delivery: 1–3 business days (select pin codes)
  • Remote Areas: 7–12 business days

Shipping Charges

Shipping is free on all orders above ₹999. A flat charge of ₹60 applies to orders below ₹999.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive an email and SMS with your tracking number. You can track your shipment directly through our courier partner's website.

Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery is unsuccessful after two attempts, the package will be held at the nearest courier facility for 5 days before being returned to us. Please ensure someone is available to receive the package.

Returns

Exchange Policy

Exchange Window

We accept exchange requests within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed, and returned in their original packaging with all tags attached.

Eligible Items for Exchange

  • Items with size issues
  • Items received in a defective or damaged condition
  • Items that significantly differ from the product description

Non-Exchangeable Items

  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Sale or discounted items (unless defective)
  • Custom-stitched or made-to-measure garments

How to Initiate an Exchange

Email hello@veloura.in with your order number, the item you wish to exchange, and the reason. Our team will respond within 24 hours with further instructions. Return shipping is covered by Veloura for defective or incorrect items.

Refunds

We currently offer exchange or store credit only. Direct refunds are issued exclusively for items lost in transit or undelivered.

Returns

Return & Refund Policy

Return Window

We accept return requests within 10 days of delivery. Items must be unused, unworn, and returned in their original packaging with all tags intact.

Eligible Items for Return

  • Items received in a defective or damaged condition
  • Items that significantly differ from the product description or image
  • Wrong item delivered

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered
  • Items without original tags or packaging
  • Sale or discounted items (unless defective)
  • Custom-stitched or made-to-measure garments
  • Innerwear, accessories, or items marked as non-returnable

How to Initiate a Return

Email ameenasaleem002@gmail.com or call 8943812820 within 10 days of delivery with:

  • Your order number
  • The item(s) you wish to return
  • Reason for return and photos (if damaged or incorrect)

Our team will respond within 24 hours with return instructions and a pickup arrangement where available.

Refund Process

  • Refunds are processed after the returned item is received and inspected
  • Approved refunds are issued as store credit or to the original payment method within 5–7 business days
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged or is incorrect, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photographs. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost.

Refund for Lost Shipments

If your order is confirmed lost in transit by the courier, we will issue a full refund or resend the order based on your preference.
